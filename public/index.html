CUMC 2024

"The greatest mathematics conference of the 21st century." - David Hilbert

Hosted at UBC.

Welcome to the CUMC 2024 website, planned to be hosted at the University of British Columbia in the beautiful Vancouver, British Columbia. If you’re seeing this page, this means you’re interested. Please bear with us while we get the website up and running. The conference is planned to be set from July 8th until July 12th 2024.

You can find registration here. Additionally, you can find lodging for the period of the single or twin rooms at Place Vanier here (ignore the Gage Suites listing).
